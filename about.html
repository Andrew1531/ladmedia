About LAD MEDIA

Founded in 2012 as an indie record label, LAD MEDIA has evolved into a full‑stack, AI‑powered marketing agency. We combine creative storytelling, modern web design, and performance‑driven strategy to help brands thrive in today’s digital landscape.

Our Journey

From music to marketing, our roots in creative culture shaped the way we approach every campaign. In 2017, we pivoted into digital strategy, building a reputation for blending artistry with measurable results.

Our Expertise

We lead paid media, SEM, and AI‑driven campaigns across platforms like Google, Meta, LinkedIn, and TikTok. Our team also specializes in SEO, brand storytelling, and modern, minimalist web experiences.

Our Vision

We’re positioning LAD MEDIA as a top‑tier, AI‑native agency in the NYC/NJ market — known for innovation, clarity, and results. Every project reflects our commitment to brand integrity and forward‑thinking design.
