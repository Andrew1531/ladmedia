Our Story

We began in 2012 as an independent record label, helping over 20 bands distribute their music globally through the internet—long before streaming dominated the industry. We mastered the art of online attention.

In 2017, we pivoted into a full-stack marketing agency, bringing our creative instincts and technical expertise to paid media, SEO, and digital strategy. Today, LAD MEDIA is an AI-native agency, integrating machine learning, predictive analytics, and generative tools into every campaign.

In 2025, we’ve expanded further—bridging AI with the world of crypto and Web3. From token launches to blockchain brand storytelling, we help clients navigate the next frontier of digital growth.
